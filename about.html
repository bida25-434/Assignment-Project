Discover The Meaning Behind The Brand

OddScope Apparel is more than just a clothing brand, it represents a mindset built around individuality, creativity, and bold self-expression. Rooted in Gaborone, the name combines uniqueness, with vision, to reflect a brand that encourages people to see the world differently and stand out from the crowd.

Grassroots Of ODDSCOPE

ODDSCOPE is a brand established in 2022 inspired by the youth culture in Gaborone. With this, the goal was to bring Urban Streetwear to Batswana

Biggest Stage Appearance

Our recognition by Celebrity World Magazine in 2025 marked a great milestone for the brand's name. This appearance amplified our brand boosting our regognition, and this informed more batswana about an amazing Apparel wear.
