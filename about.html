ODDSCOPE Apparel 2022

OddScope Apparel is more than just a clothing brand, it represents a mindset built around individuality, creativity, and bold self-expression. Rooted in Gaborone, the name combines “odd,” symbolizing uniqueness, with “scope,” meaning vision, to reflect a brand that encourages people to see the world differently and stand out from the crowd. Since emerging around 2024/2025, OddScope has grown into a streetwear label, offering hoodies, tees, and winter wear. With strong ties to local culture, Setswana slang, and youth-driven energy, the brand speaks directly to creatives who want to build their own identity. Its recognition by Celebrity World Magazine marked it as a rising force in Botswana’s urban fashion scene, reinforcing its mission: not just to sell apparel, but to help people bring their vision to life and proudly represent their own wave
